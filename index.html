It works by transmitting data between a satellite dish installed at the user's location and an orbiting satellite, which then communicates with a network operations center on the ground. This technology is particularly relevant in the telecom and network management industry because it offers connectivity solutions in remote and underserved areas where traditional wired or wireless services are unavailable.
